## 6. Salary Negotiation Guide

### Research Your Market Value

**Use These Resources:**
- Levels.fyi (actual salaries by company/role)
- Salary.com (US-wide ranges)
- Blind (anonymous tech salaries)
- Glassdoor (company reviews with salary)
- Github (salary repo by location)

### Calculate Your Target Range

```
Years Experience: 3 years
Role: React Developer
Location: San Francisco
Base Comp: $140K-$180K (from research)
Company: Series B startup

Range calculation:
  Low: $140K (25th percentile)
  High: $180K (75th percentile)
  Target: $165K (60th percentile)

Total Comp Target: $240K-$280K
  Includes: stock, bonus, benefits
```

### Negotiation Strategy

1. **Research First** (2-3 weeks before negotiation)
   - Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, Salary.com
   - Ask peers (anonymously if needed)
   - Use salary calculators

2. **Know Your Value**
   - Skills, experience, achievements
   - Years in role
   - Unique strengths
   - Market demand for skills

3. **Name Your Number Last**
   - Get their offer first
   - Ask for total comp range
   - Request time to consider

4. **Counter Respectfully**
   - Provide data (research links)
   - Focus on value you bring
   - Be willing to negotiate total comp
   - Consider stock options, bonus, benefits

5. **Be Flexible**
   - Low base + high stock (startup)
   - Higher base + lower stock (public)
   - Sign-on bonus
   - PTO and benefits
   - Work location flexibility

## 11. Long-Term Wealth Building

### Stock Options & Equity

**Startup Equity (Typical):**
- Entry-level: 0.05-0.2%
- Mid-level: 0.1-0.5%
- Senior: 0.2-1.0%
- Staff: 0.5-2.0%

**Expected Outcomes:**
- Successful exit: 3-10x return
- Moderate success: 1-2x return
- Failure: 0% return

**Strategy:**
- Prefer equity at earlier stage companies
- Negotiate for higher equity if lower salary
- Diversify across multiple startups

### Public Company Stock

**Typical Packages:**
- $20K-$50K for entry-level
- $50K-$150K for mid-level
- $150K-$500K for senior
- $500K-$5M+ for staff/principal

**4-Year Vesting:**
- Cliff: 25% after 1 year
- Monthly: 1/48 monthly for years 2-4

**Strategy:**
- Build wealth over 4 years
- Negotiate initial grant
- Negotiate annual refreshes
