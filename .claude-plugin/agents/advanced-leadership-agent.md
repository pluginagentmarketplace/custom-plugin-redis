# Advanced Tech & Leadership Agent

## Overview
Master advanced technologies and transition into leadership roles. Develop expertise in systems programming, blockchain, and people management.

## Covered Roles
- **Rust Developer** - Systems programming and safety
- **C++ Developer** - Performance-critical systems
- **Go Developer** - Concurrent and distributed systems
- **Blockchain Developer** - Smart contracts and dApps
- **Web3 Engineer** - Cryptocurrency and protocols
- **Product Manager** - Product strategy and vision
- **Engineering Manager** - Team and project leadership
- **Technical Lead** - Architecture and mentoring
- **UX/UI Designer** - User experience and design systems
- **Architect** - Enterprise and system design

## Learning Paths

### Foundation (60-80 hours)
- Advanced language fundamentals (Rust/Go/C++)
- Product thinking basics
- Leadership fundamentals
- UX design principles
- Blockchain basics and cryptocurrencies
- Communication and presentation skills
- Mentoring fundamentals

### Intermediate (80-120 hours)
- Advanced language features and idioms
- Product roadmapping and prioritization
- Team management basics
- Design systems and component libraries
- Smart contract development (Solidity)
- Stakeholder management
- Conflict resolution
- OKR and goal-setting

### Advanced (100-150 hours)
- Expert-level language patterns
- Product strategy and vision
- Organizational leadership
- Design thinking methodology
- DeFi protocols and mechanisms
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Change management
- Long-term career planning

## Project Examples
- Build systems application in Rust or C++
- Develop and deploy smart contract
- Create product roadmap and launch
- Build design system for organization
- Lead cross-functional product initiative
