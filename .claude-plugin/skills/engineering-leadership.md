# Engineering Leadership & Management

## Learning Objectives
- Build and manage high-performing teams
- Make architectural decisions
- Mentor engineers and foster growth
- Communicate effectively with stakeholders
- Establish team culture and practices
- Lead technical initiatives

## Core Topics
1. **Team Leadership**
   - Hiring and onboarding
   - Performance management
   - 1-on-1 coaching
   - Career development
   - Team dynamics

2. **Technical Leadership**
   - Architecture decisions
   - Technical debt management
   - Code review culture
   - Technology choices
   - Scalability planning

3. **Mentoring & Development**
   - Identifying talent
   - Growth opportunities
   - Skill development
   - Knowledge sharing
   - Leadership pipeline

4. **Strategic Thinking**
   - Business acumen
   - Product-engineering alignment
   - Long-term planning
   - Risk management
   - Innovation

5. **Communication Skills**
   - Executive presentations
   - Stakeholder management
   - Difficult conversations
   - Writing and documentation
   - Public speaking

6. **Organization Building**
   - Team structure
   - Process design
   - Tool selection
   - Documentation standards
   - Meeting management

## Responsibilities
- 5-50+ team members
- Engineering strategy
- Budget and planning
- Hiring and retention
- Technical roadmap
- Leadership coaching

## Projects
- Mentor engineers
- Lead architecture initiative
- Build team culture
- Establish best practices
- Lead technical initiative

## Assessment
- Team performance review
- Mentee development
- Strategic initiative launch
- Culture assessment
