# Product Management & Strategy

## Learning Objectives
- Define product vision and strategy
- Conduct user research and validation
- Build and prioritize roadmaps
- Analyze metrics and KPIs
- Lead cross-functional teams
- Launch and iterate on products

## Core Topics
1. **Product Strategy**
   - Vision and mission
   - Market analysis
   - Competitive landscape
   - Strategic positioning
   - OKRs and KPIs

2. **User Research**
   - User interviews
   - Surveys and questionnaires
   - Personas and jobs to be done
   - User testing
   - Analytics and data analysis

3. **Product Roadmapping**
   - Roadmap formats
   - Prioritization frameworks
   - Release planning
   - Feature scoping
   - Capacity planning

4. **Metrics & Analytics**
   - KPIs and success metrics
   - Funnel analysis
   - Retention and engagement
   - A/B testing
   - Data-driven decisions

5. **Cross-Functional Leadership**
   - Stakeholder management
   - Engineering collaboration
   - Design partnership
   - Communication
   - Decision making

## Frameworks & Tools
- OKR methodology
- RICE prioritization
- Jobs to be Done
- Jobs Stories format
- Intercom, Amplitude, Mixpanel
- Figma for collaboration
- Jira, Linear, Asana

## Projects
- Product strategy document
- User research findings
- Product roadmap
- Metrics dashboard
- Launch plan

## Assessment
- Develop product strategy
- Conduct user research
- Build prioritized roadmap
- Launch feature analysis
